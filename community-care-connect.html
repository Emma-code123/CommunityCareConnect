About Community Care Connect

Community Care Connect was founded by Emma Gershenson, a high school freshman passionate about biomedical engineering and improving healthcare access.

Recognizing that many people struggle to navigate the complex healthcare system and find the resources they need, Emma created this platform to connect community members with vital healthcare services, support programs, and health education.

What started as a local initiative in Hudson, Massachusetts, is growing to serve communities across the state and beyond. Our mission is simple: ensure everyone has access to the health resources they need, regardless of their background or circumstances.

Our Mission

To bridge the gap between healthcare resources and the people who need them, creating healthier, more informed communities through accessible information and compassionate connection.
